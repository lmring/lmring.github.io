Attributes

Attributes describe different things about your character.

Try changing Hank's numeric value for each attribute and see what happens!

Melee fighters who rely on weapons
  • Barbarians
  • Fighters

Dexterity

Hank is the CLUMSIEST MAN ALIVE!

Hank probably actually has two left feet!

Hank is not too clumsy, not too agile!

Hank is pretty good on his feet!

Hank is SPIDERHANK!

Dexterity is useful for:

Ranged weapons users such as:
  • Fighters
  • Rangers
Spellcasters who have ranged weapons as their alternate way of doing damage Finesse weapons user such as:
  • Bards
  • Monks
    • When a monk uses a monk weapon, they use dexterity.
  • Rogues
Armor Class
  • Depending on the armor you wear, your dexterity modifier may or may not come into play.
  • Barbarians have their unarmored defense decided by their dexterity and consitution.
  • Monks have their unarmored defense decided by their dexterity and wisdom.
Having high initiative
  • Everytime you go into battle, you roll initiative, which uses your dex modifier.
  • The higher the initiative, the earlier your turn to attack is.
The following checks:
  • Enduring certain magical spells
  • Having quick reflexes or a good reaction time
  • Disabling a trap
The following skills:
  • Acrobatics
  • Stealth
  • Sleight of Hand

Hank is a pretty tough dude, who can put up with most stuff!

Constitution is useful for:

Anyone who is going to be in the front lines, especially tanks, such as:
  • Barbarians
  • Monks
  • Fighters
  • Paladins
Determining your hit points
  • Your constitution modifier will be added to all hit points you get.
Barbarian's unarmored defense is determined by dexterity and constitution. The following checks:
  • Holding your breath
  • Going without rest or sleep
  • Drinking alcohol...hic!
  • Resisting poison attacks
