Artist Bio

Lauren Ring (she/her, aka Finchie of @rosefinchie) is a lesbian artist and merchandise designer who has created dozens of enamel pins, including three successfully funded Kickstarter sets. She has recently designed merchandise for Neon Hemlock Press, and has contributed illustrations to independent zines such as Soft and Warm, Carpe Noctem, and Classics But Make It Gay.
